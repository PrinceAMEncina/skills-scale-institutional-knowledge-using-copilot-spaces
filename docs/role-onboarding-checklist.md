# OctoAcme Role Onboarding Checklist

## Purpose
This checklist helps new team members quickly understand their role, responsibilities, and how they interact with other project team members.

## General Onboarding (All Roles)
- [ ] Review [Project Management Overview](octoacme-project-management-overview.md)
- [ ] Read [Roles and Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) document
- [ ] Understand project lifecycle phases (Initiation → Planning → Execution → Release → Retrospective)
- [ ] Access project repository and documentation
- [ ] Join relevant communication channels (Slack, Teams, email lists)
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager for project overview
- [ ] Review current project status and timeline
- [ ] Understand escalation paths and communication protocols

## Role-Specific Onboarding

### For Developers
- [ ] Review codebase structure and coding standards
- [ ] Set up development environment
- [ ] Understand CI/CD pipeline and testing requirements
- [ ] Review acceptance criteria and Definition of Done
- [ ] Participate in code review process
- [ ] Attend daily standups and sprint planning

### For Product Managers
- [ ] Review product vision and roadmap
- [ ] Meet with key stakeholders
- [ ] Understand success metrics and business objectives
- [ ] Review customer research and feedback
- [ ] Access product analytics and metrics dashboards
- [ ] Review backlog prioritization approach

### For Project Managers
- [ ] Review project charter and one-pager
- [ ] Access project boards and tracking tools
- [ ] Review risk register and mitigation plans
- [ ] Understand resource allocation and constraints
- [ ] Meet with Project Sponsor and stakeholders
- [ ] Review communication plan and cadence

### For Quality Assurance Leads
- [ ] Review quality standards and testing strategy
- [ ] Understand acceptance criteria and test coverage requirements
- [ ] Access test environments and tools
- [ ] Review defect tracking process
- [ ] Meet with Developers and Product Managers
- [ ] Understand release quality gates

### For Business Analysts
- [ ] Review existing requirements documentation
- [ ] Understand business objectives and success criteria
- [ ] Meet with stakeholders and Product Managers
- [ ] Review user stories and acceptance criteria format
- [ ] Access requirements management tools
- [ ] Understand validation and sign-off process

### For Project Sponsors
- [ ] Review strategic alignment and business case
- [ ] Understand funding and resource commitments
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager and Product Manager
- [ ] Review escalation protocols
- [ ] Understand milestone approval process
- [ ] Schedule regular steering committee meetings

### For Change Managers
- [ ] Review change management plan
- [ ] Understand stakeholder landscape and impact assessment
- [ ] Access adoption metrics and feedback tools
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager and team leads
- [ ] Review training and communication materials
- [ ] Understand change readiness approach

### For Subject Matter Experts (SMEs)
- [ ] Understand domain or technical area of expertise needed
- [ ] Review project scope and technical approach
- [ ] Identify key decision points requiring expert input
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager and technical leads
- [ ] Understand consultation and review process
- [ ] Review compliance or regulatory requirements (if applicable)

## First Week Goals
- [ ] Complete all general onboarding items
- [ ] Complete role-specific onboarding items
- [ ] Shadow at least one meeting or work session
- [ ] Introduce yourself to the team
- [ ] Identify your first contribution or deliverable
- [ ] Schedule regular 1:1s with key collaborators

## 30-Day Goals
- [ ] Demonstrate understanding of your role and responsibilities
- [ ] Actively contribute to project deliverables
- [ ] Build working relationships with cross-functional team members
- [ ] Provide feedback on onboarding process for continuous improvement

## Questions or Issues?
Contact your Project Manager or team lead for support during onboarding.
